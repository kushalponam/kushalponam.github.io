VoltCity is an Educational game aimed at teaching it's players about differect sources of energy available at Madeira,Portugal. Player's Objective in the game is to provide electricity to the city in demand, while collecting electricity from the powerplant. In this process, the player learns about renewable and non-renewable resources and how they are effected by seasonal changes.

VolCity is a multiplayer game, played on a 40 inch touch screen table. This game is currently installed at Casa da Luz, Electricity Museum at Madeira,Portugal.
